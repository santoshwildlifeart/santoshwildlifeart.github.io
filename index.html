Homepage - My Wildlife Art

NOTE: This site is under construction and does not yet have most of its intended content or design. It will be finished and the first dozen paintings uploaded by Christmas 2017, so if you have stumbled by here, please visit again in a month's time!

Welcome! This site is primarily for hosting my wildlife art in oils, watercolour, acrylic, pastel and pencil. My landscape paintings and botanical art will also be added in due course.

My passion and aspiration are to express through painting the sense of beauty I perceive when confronted with Earth's wilderness and wildlife, and to enable you to feel it too. A second but equally important desire is to contribute to wildlife conservation efforts worldwide through sale proceeds from my art. To this end, 50% of the sale price of any of my paintings will be donated to a wildlife conservation organisation. In this way I hope to repay in a small measure for the joy and beauty I have enjoyed from watching and painting wildlife.

You can access the main sections of the site through the menu above. The Gallery page collects all my wildlife paintings and new addtions to this site are noted in the What's new page. You can also read more about me on the About page. Finally all my non-wildlife art related interests are behind the Other Interests section.

I hope this site and my paintings and drawings inspire you to feel the beauty of Earth's wildlife and the vital urgency to cherish and protect it. Thank you for stopping by!
